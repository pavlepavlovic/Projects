About Us

Our cat adoption team is a non-profit organization, dedicated to the welfare of cats, located in Serbia, Bosnia and Germany. For more than 10 years we are helping cats in need. We operate with the help of volunteers and donations. We adopt out cats to responsible homes only and we always provide lifelong support to our adopters. We are happy to help you if you have any questions, or if you want to join our team of volunteers.
